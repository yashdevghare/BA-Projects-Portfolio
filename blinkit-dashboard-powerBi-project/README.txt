# BlinkIT Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing BlinkIT’s sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution using **Power BI**.  
The goal was to build an interactive dashboard that highlights **key KPIs** and provides actionable insights for sales optimization, outlet management, and customer experience improvement.  

## 🎯 Business Requirement
To conduct a comprehensive analysis of BlinkIT’s operations to identify trends and opportunities for growth by evaluating sales performance, customer satisfaction, and outlet-level distribution.  

## 📊 KPI Metrics
1. **Total Sales** – Overall revenue generated from items sold  
2. **Average Sales** – Average revenue per sales transaction  
3. **Number of Items** – Total count of items sold  
4. **Average Rating** – Average customer satisfaction rating  

## 📈 Dashboard Features
1. **Total Sales by Fat Content** – Donut chart to analyze impact of fat content on sales  
2. **Total Sales by Item Type** – Bar chart to identify best & worst performing item types  
3. **Fat Content by Outlet (Sales)** – Stacked column chart comparing sales by fat content across outlets  
4. **Sales by Outlet Establishment** – Line chart showing impact of outlet age/type on sales  
5. **Sales by Outlet Size** – Pie chart analyzing correlation between outlet size and sales  
6. **Sales by Outlet Location** – Pie chart showing geographic distribution of sales  
7. **All KPIs by Outlet Type** – Multi-metric breakdown (Sales, Avg. Sales, Items, Ratings) by outlet type  

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI** (Data modeling, DAX, and visualization)  
- **Excel** (Dataset preprocessing)  

## 📌 Key Insights
- Certain **item types and fat content** significantly drive sales performance.  
- **Outlet size and establishment year** impact total revenue generation.  
- **Geographic differences** in sales highlight potential regional strategies.  
- KPI comparisons across **outlet types** reveal strengths and weaknesses for expansion.
